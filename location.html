Getting Here

How to Find Us

No matter how you travel, we're easy to reach. Here are the best ways to get to Java Buzz.

🚇

By Subway

Take the A, C, or E line to 14th Street Station. We're a 3-minute walk south on 8th Avenue, then right on Brew Street.

🚗

By Car

Take I-95 to exit 12, then follow 9th Avenue south. Turn right on Brew Street. Parking available on-street and in the 5th Ave garage.

🚶

On Foot

From Penn Station, head west on 34th Street to 9th Ave, then south 6 blocks. Turn left on Brew Street — look for our green awning!
